Case Study Generator

Upload a completed HCD project report (.pptx) and AI will extract the content into a structured 13-slide case study template for you to review and copy.

⚠️ API key not configured. to add your LiteLLM key before uploading.
How it works
  1. Upload your completed HCD project report (.pptx) — it's read entirely in your browser, nothing is uploaded to a server.
  2. The tool extracts text from each slide, then sends it to the AI in 3 focused batches.
  3. Review the structured output below. Fields marked [NOT FOUND] need manual input.
  4. Copy individual slides or the full output to paste into your case study template.
  5. Note: Slides 9–11 (About the SDC Team, Ways of Working, Vision) are standard boilerplate — fill these from your own template.
